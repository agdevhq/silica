---
title: Table of contents
description: In-page heading navigation on wide screens.
---

On viewports `lg` and wider, a table of contents appears in the right column, sticky as you scroll.

## What appears in the ToC

Headings `h1` through `h6` from the rendered page content. Each entry links to the heading anchor.

Headings also get wrap-around link icons (click the heading to copy the anchor URL).

## When it is hidden

The ToC column is hidden on smaller screens to keep the reading column full-width. Use the vault tree and search for navigation on mobile.
