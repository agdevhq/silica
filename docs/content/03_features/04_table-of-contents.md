---
title: Table of contents
description: In-page heading navigation on wide screens.
---

On wide screens, a table of contents appears in the right column and stays in view as you scroll. It is built from the headings on the page, and each entry jumps to its heading.

Hovering a heading also reveals a link icon — click it to copy a direct link to that section.

On narrower screens the table of contents is hidden to keep the reading column full-width; use the sidebar and search to get around instead.
